About

Katherine Billings

I'm Katherine Billings, but you can just call me Katie or Kate. I have a lot of nicknames. I'm 20 years old and from Mississauga, Ontario. But, to put it simply Toronto, cause not many people know where Mississauga is. I'm a student in the Interactive Media Design program at Fanshawe College in London, Ontario. I learned about both design and development and much more. I lean more towards the coding part of it, but I've been working on my art skills and design. As my electives, I've delved into mythology, fairy tales and legends in my Myth, Folktale and Fairy Tale class, and also comedy films in my comedy class. I'm open to learning new things and am rather empathetic.

I'm also a huge geek and love anime, movies, sci-fi and games. I can go into long talks and debates about things I'm passionate about. I also like to sing and dance, and recently I've taken to writing some stories.
